Creating a masterpiece

Wine is a masterpiece that is created by daily painstaking work. To make an outstanding wine, you cannot do without the experience of many generations, good vines and great love for your work.

Non-standard gastronomic combinations for sherry .

Sherry is a special type of wine, a whole section, a separate continent, to which we do not pay as much attention as other alcoholic beverages, which are more universal and widespread. However, in Andalusia there is no more versatile drink than sherry.

Sparks of mulled wine flaming in a glass.

This drink is great for drinking in the cold winter, after a walk in the frosty air. Mulled wine is a traditional companion of Christmas markets and outdoor festivities.

Rose-scented: styles of Muscat wine

It is probably difficult to find a more recognizable grape variety than Muscat. Wines from it are fragrant with tones of roses, raisins, sweet citruses and ripe pears. Despite its flamboyant character, it has become a fairly common variety from which a wide range of wines are made. Today we will try to understand the styles of wines from this fragrant variety.
